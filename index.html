About the data

🟢 Live data — Istanbul (Kapalıçarşı Grand Bazaar): Real buy/sell prices from the Istanbul physical gold market, updated in real time.

🟠 Calculated data — Shanghai (SGE), Mumbai (MCX/IBJA), Tokyo (TOCOM), Dubai, Jakarta (Antam): These prices are calculated as international spot price × local currency exchange rate, plus known local premiums where applicable. They represent an approximation of the local price.

For Shanghai the typical SGE premium over COMEX has historically ranged from −$10 to +$50/oz depending on Chinese demand and PBOC import restrictions. For India a ~13% premium applies due to 15% import duty + 3% GST. For Istanbul the actual premium varies 0–5% depending on local demand — use the live Kapalıçarşı data for the real price.

Data sources: gold-api.com (spot) · frankfurter.app (FX) · Kapalıçarşı API (Istanbul)
